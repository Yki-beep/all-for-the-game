David Wymack

David Vincent Wymack is the Foxes' coach.
He is a large man, with tribal flame tattoos. He has brown eyes, and is often wearing a white wife beater and jeans.
Wymack puts up a tough act in front of the Foxes, but he genuinely cares about them. He deals with their antics using both threats and sarcasm.
Wymack repeatedly tells his team members to not over-exert themselves on the court, especially if they are injured. He would rather lose a game than have a player indisposed for future games.
Coach Wymack founded the Foxes to give people from difficult backgrounds another chance. Contrary to popular belief, this isn't a publicity stunt. Wymack is seen as idealistic for this.
To ensure the safety of his players, he goes beyond what a coach is supposed to do and even houses some players temporarily in his or Abby's appartment. He is protective of them and offers to help Neil if the cousins and Kevin pose too much of a problem, but mostly keeps out of the team's personal affairs.

Abby Winfield

Abby Marie Winfield is the Foxes' nurse. She is decisive and has no problem to shut people up or order them around. Foxes usualy joke about Wymack's and Abby's relationship, but they deny it.

Betsy Dobson

Betsy Jo Dobson is the Foxes' psychiatrist.
Betsy's hair is pale brown and reaches to her chin. She has "a few extra curves" and laugh lines on her face. She wears narrow-rimmed glasses.
It is mandatory for the team to see her once a semester, but are free to visit her whenever they feel the need. This is included in the Foxes' tuition fees. She allows her patients to call her whatever they want, from "Betsy" to "Doc" to "Hey You".

#1: Dan Wilds

Danielle Leigh "Dan" Wilds is the captain of the Foxes and a 4th year at Palmetto State University. She is passionate about the Foxes and doesn't tolerate her teammates dragging them down by talking bad about the team.

Appearance

Her brown hair is cut “mercilessly short” in response to “nagging from the men” in her freshman year. She has brown eyes. Her smile was once described as “a toothy grin that was equal parts menace and mirth.” She is about 5'4"-5'7"/162-170cm tall.

Personality

Dan is passionate and adamant in enforcing the rules she sets. She wants the team to function (or at least not to kill one another) and fully acts her role as captain with conviction. She tries to include everyone in activities.
Just like the other members Dan loves to participate in the bets.
Dan majored in Athletic Training with a minor in Coaching

Dan worked as a stripper in high school as a way of funding her home life. Her stage name was Hennessey, named for the hard liquor. Dan was signed to the Foxes in their second year and immediately became team captain. She (and Wymack) persevered and slowly her team fell in behind her.

#2: Kevin Day

Kevin Day is the starting striker of the Palmetto State Foxes. He was formerly a member of the Edgar Allan Ravens and was considered one of the best contemporary Exy players alongside his adoptive brother Riko Moriyama until an injury to his dominant hand forced to relearn to play with his right hand.
Along with Riko, Kevin is often called "The Son of Exy" and "The Future of Exy,"

Appearance

Kevin has green eyes and dark hair, and is about 6' to 6'2. Kevin has a tattoo on his left cheekbone.

Personality

Kevin was describes as arrogant, spineless, and Exy-obsessed. Because of his traumatic upbringing, Kevin most likely suffers from anxiety and PTSD. He's also made fun for being a 'healthy nut' when actually this can also be a result from his time with the Ravens and it's most likely an eating disorder.
Because of Tetsuji and Riko, Kevin grew up with the mentality that Exy is the most important thing someone can have, which causes him to be merciless when it comes to the game. He has really high standards when it comes to Exy players and is not afraid to point other people faults. This, at times, causes people to get angry and snap at him.
Even though hess shown as someone cold and egoistic, he has his own ways of caring for others

#3: Andrew Minyard

Andrew Joseph Minyard is a sophomore Criminal Justice major at Palmetto State University, and a goalie for the PSU Foxes Exy team. He is known for his ferocity both on and off the court.

Appearance

Andrew has blonde hair, pale skin and hazel eyes. He always wears black wristbands that reach from his wrists to his elbows. They are rumored to be a sarcastic attempt to differentiate him from his twin, Aaron, for other people's sake. In reality, he is hiding the scars on his wrists from years of self-harm, and a convenient sheath for knives. At 5' flat, he is very short - his Exy racquet is taller than he is - and "doesn't look like much" in person, however, he is also considered a person to be afraid of by the majority of The Foxes.

Personality

Andrew keeps his promises, almost to a fault. He will go very far out of his way to protect what's his. His moods generally range from apathy to violence, exceptions that allow differences in his attitude include Renee, Aaron, Kevin, Nicky, and Neil. His cold and heartless exterior is to hide his trauma and insecurities. He is dedicated to upholding a strict set of personal values and is wholly protective of the the people he cares for. Despite these sentiments, he outwardly appears to have little to no regard for his own life.

#10: Neil Josten

Neil Abram Josten or Nathaniel Abram Wesninski(birth name) is a striker for the Palmetto State Foxes, tho he can also play defense, since it was the first role he ever played.

Appearance

Neil is described as a short(5'3"/160 cm) and athletic young adult. When he just joined Foxes Neil dyes his hair black and wears brown contact lenses. His hair is naturally auburn and his true eye color is a chilly shade of blue. He is also attractive, as seen with comments from Nicky and getting hit on by Marissa. His wardrobe consists mainly of worn, basic pieces of clothing, such as t-shirts, jeans, and sneakers. He does not have a particular fashion sense, valuing utility over anything else, and chooses his attire based on its ability to cover his scars and make him appear average.

Personality

Neil is perceptive, a trait necessary for his life on the run. When Andrew picks him up from the airport under the guise of being Aaron, Neil quickly figures out that he isn't actually Aaron, and that he's off his prescribed drugs.
Neil doesn't trust other people. He doesn't accept their offers of help and tries to solve every problem on his own, and he is generally closed off and secretive about himself. Neil also attempts to constantly stay in control of the situation.
Neil is almost as obsessed with Exy as Kevin is, although he had to stay away from it most of his life. This obsession makes him risk everything to play for the Foxes.
He doesn't shy away from a fight. He is good at starting fights but not at winning them.
Neil has a certain control over his emotions and can shove down his anger when it interferes with his survival.
